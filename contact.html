Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about my services, process, and collaboration.

What services do you offer?

I offer a comprehensive range of services including web development, mobile app development, UI/UX design, SEO optimization, database design, API development, and AI/machine learning solutions. You can find detailed information about each service on my Services page.

How much do your services cost?

My pricing is project-based and varies depending on the scope, complexity, and requirements. I offer different packages starting from $499 for basic websites. For a personalized quote tailored to your specific needs, please contact me with details about your project.

How long does it take to complete a project?

Project timelines depend on complexity and scope. A simple website typically takes 2-3 weeks, while more complex applications can take several months. During our initial consultation, I'll provide you with a detailed timeline and milestones for your specific project.

Do you offer maintenance services after project completion?

Yes, I offer comprehensive maintenance packages to ensure your website or application continues to run smoothly after launch. These packages include regular updates, security patches, performance optimization, and ongoing technical support.

What is your development process?

My development process follows an agile methodology: Discovery & Planning → Design & Prototyping → Development → Testing & Quality Assurance → Deployment → Post-launch Support. I maintain clear communication throughout each phase.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! I work with clients from around the world. I'm experienced in remote collaboration and use modern communication tools to ensure smooth project delivery regardless of time zones or geographical location.
