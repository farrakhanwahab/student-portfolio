Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

I offer a range of services including web development, mobile app development, SEO optimization, database design, API development, and AI/machine learning solutions. You can find more details on my Services page.

How much do your services cost?

My pricing varies depending on the scope and requirements of each project. I offer different packages starting from $499 for basic websites. For a personalized quote, please contact me with details about your project.

Yes, I offer maintenance packages to ensure your website or application continues to run smoothly after launch. These packages include regular updates, security patches, and technical support.
